Informed Consent Generator

Create a study-specific informed consent form for HCI research directly in your browser. The generator helps you describe the purpose, procedure, compensation, recorded data and privacy conditions of your study, then produces a printable consent document in HTML and PDF without requiring a backend service.

This tool is intended for standard HCI user studies and student research workflows. It is not a substitute for legal review, ethics consultation or supervisory guidance, especially for medical contexts, vulnerable groups or high-risk interventions.

About This Tool

What the generator helps with

This informed consent generator is built for HCI user studies, usability tests, interviews, lab studies, online studies, and related student research projects. It helps research teams document study purpose, procedure, compensation, data collection, anonymization, retention, and publication decisions in a structured consent form.

The app is especially useful for researchers, supervisors, and students who need a fast starting point for a consent form template for human-computer interaction research. It runs fully in the browser, supports HTML preview, local PDF export, and reusable saved settings without a server backend.

Frequently asked questions

What is this app for?
It helps create informed consent forms for HCI and user research studies.

Does it work offline?
Yes. The generator is designed to run locally in the browser without requiring a backend service.

Can I export the result?
Yes. The generated consent form can be reviewed as HTML and exported as PDF locally.
